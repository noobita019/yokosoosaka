How to Order

1

Browse & Add to Cart

Browse our catalog, select your preferred color and size, then add items to your cart.

2

Create Your Account

Fill in your name, contact number, email, and delivery address so we can process your order.

3

Review & Place Order

Review your order summary, then place your order. You'll get a PO number for reference.

4

Pay Deposit & Wait

Pay the deposit amount to confirm your order. We ship from Japan to PH and will update you on the ETA.

About Us

JapanGoodies is a pasabuy service based in Davao City and Quezon City. We bring authentic Japanese products — from GU/Uniqlo fashion to cosmetics, shoes, and more — straight from Japan to your doorstep.
